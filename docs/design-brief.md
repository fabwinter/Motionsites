# Design Brief

## Subject grounding

Motionsites sells premium 3D animated websites for agencies, studios, and creators. The visual world should feel cinematic, glassy, and engineered rather than decorative.

## Tokens

- Colors: `#050816`, `#0f1530`, `#7ee7ff`, `#9fb0ff`, `#d7e1ff`, `#ff8a7a`
- Display: Space Grotesk
- Body: Inter
- Utility: JetBrains Mono
- Spacing scale: 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 80

## Signature

A floating glass form in an animated gradient hero, paired with one pinned production-system scroll sequence.

## Anti-defaults

- Avoid generic particle-sphere visuals.
- Avoid flat SaaS gradients with no materiality.
- Keep the rest of the page restrained once the hero and process sequence land.

## Copy direction

Use direct, production-minded language. Sell the workflow and polish, not vague creative buzzwords.
